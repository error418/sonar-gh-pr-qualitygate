Integrate and observe the results of your CI/CD pipeline tools

More transparency, less hassle.

Swingletree processes reports generated by your CI/CD tooling, transforms them into a developer-readable format, applies defined quality gates and attaches the information directly to your commits and Pull-Requests. Developers do not need to scroll through endless build logs to find the cause of a failed build.
